School Uniforms

When a company wants more than all of our main options, we offer our service of sewing. Sometimes organizations like schools, universities, sport teams and companies have only a design of their logo, but they do not know how their uniform can be. Based on a logo we can sew a custom uniform. All uniforms are going to be different for each company, because all the logos are different and unique.

Marching Bands

Now, when it comes to creating uniforms for marching bands we offer the service of sewing as well. Uniforms for marching bands require more work and process, but we are willing to provide the best service. Even when a marching band does not bring a custom design or idea for a uniform, with the colors of the band it is possible to create something new.
